Hat Tutorial

Brown Bear Hat

This hat doubles the resources gained when collecting from bushes and rocks. This only applies to the person who is wearing the hat, friendly peasants and mercenaries will not be affected. Obtainable through brown bears.

Chicken Hat

When wearing this hat, the player is hidden from the minimap. Be aware that player friendly mercenaries and peasants will not be hidden. Obtainable through chickens at night.

Cowboy Hat

The cool down time required to start regenerating health is shortened when this hat is equipped, it is much faster regeneration. Obtainable through ice monsters during day and wolves during day.

Druid / Tree hat

Animals won't attack you. Obtainable through pigs.

This is for normal animals(Wolves, Brown Bears, and Trolls) not winter animals.

Builder Hat

When a player is wearing this hat, destroying any player constructed blocks (wooden walls, stone walls) will yield 50% more resources. Useful for reconstructing bases or dismantling enemy blocks. Obtainable through pigs and chickens.

Fox Hat

Players wearing this hat can see others in the night, but its a small radius. Obtainable through wolves at night.

Hockey Hat

If you are wearing this hat when you die, you will regain 1/3 of your health, but lose the hat. Obtainable through hammer monsters, ice monsters and bears during day and night.

Ice Hat

Icemen won’t attack you wearing this hat. Obtainable through killing the icemen.

Golden Ice Hat

More gold, but icemen will attack you and do more damage. Obtainable through killing the icemen.

White Bear Hat

Players wearing this hat destroy player placed blocks twice as fast. Obtainable through killing the hammer men.

Speed Hat

Makes you faster. Obtainable through pigs during day.

Ninja Hat

Faster and nearly invisible, especially at night. You don’t notice pain, so be careful of dying! Also invisible on the minimap, obtainable through chickens at night and it is a 1/1000 chance.

Tank Hat

Players wearing this hat does 75% more damage and takes only 25% of damage and speed gets reduced to 50%. HP is also increased very well. Obtainable by destroying spikes at night.

Viking Hat

Players wearing this hat do slightly more damage (25%) to enemy players and NPCs. Obtainable through killing icemen at night and wolves at night.

Trojan Hat

Players wearing this hat will allow you to do slight more damage (20%) and walk slightly faster (10%). Obtainable through killing trolls

Vampire Hat

Players wearing this hat will regenerate slightly (20% of health) with every hit done to an enemy player or entity

Admin Hat

Allows players walk through walls, speed increase, defense increase, faster attack, “its the impossible”.
